# 1. Product Overview

ColorGifts is a web application that allows users to upload personal photos and automatically convert them into printable coloring books. Users can preview, customize, and order a physical book delivered to their home or download a printable PDF.

The product focuses on **emotional gifting experiences**, not just AI image generation.

Primary output formats:
- Printed coloring book (via Printify or Lulu)
- Digital PDF download

---

# 2. Problem Statement

People have thousands of meaningful personal photos (kids, pets, memories) but:
- They are passive (stored in phones, rarely used)
- Photo books are static and expensive
- Existing AI tools do not produce physical, gift-ready experiences

There is no simple product that transforms personal memories into **interactive, creative coloring books**.

---

# 3. Target Users

## Primary Audience (MVP Focus)
- Parents (especially mothers with children ages 3–8)
- Looking for educational + creative activities
- Buy personalized gifts regularly

## Secondary Audiences
- Pet owners (turn pets into coloring books)
- Couples (anniversaries, memories)
- Grandparents (family memory gifts)

---

# 4. Core Value Proposition

> “Turn your favorite memories into a personalized coloring book in minutes.”

Key emotional drivers:
- Personalization

- Nostalgia

---

# 5. MVP Scope

## Must-Have Features

### 5.1 Landing Page
- Hero section with before/after transformation (photo → coloring page → printed book)
- Clear CTA: “Create Your Coloring Book”
- Simple explanation of process (3 steps max)

---

### 5.2 Photo Upload System
- Drag & drop upload
- Support multiple images (5–20 recommended)
- Mobile-first upload experience
- Basic validation (image size/type)

---

### 5.3 Style Selection
Users choose output style:

- Simple (Ages 3–5)
- Cartoon Style (Ages 5–8)
- Detailed (Older kids/adults)

This affects image processing prompt.

---

### 5.4 AI Coloring Page Generation
System converts uploaded photos into:
- Clean black-and-white line drawings
- Printable coloring page format
- Consistent style across pages

Output requirements:
- High contrast
- No grayscale shading
- No noise or artifacts
- Consistent stroke thickness

---

### 5.5 Preview Gallery
- Show generated pages before purchase
- Allow re-generation (basic retry button)
- Display as flipbook or grid view

---

### 5.6 Book Builder
- Auto-layout pages into book format
- Add:
  - Title (user-defined)
  - Subtitle (optional)
  - Dedication page
- Preview full book structure

---

### 5.7 Checkout System
Two purchase options:

1. Digital PDF download
2. Printed book (physical delivery)

Payment via Stripe.

Pricing (MVP suggestion):
- PDF: $9.99
- Printed book: $19.99–$29.99

---

### 5.8 Fulfillment Integration
Use print-on-demand service:
- Printify API OR Lulu API

Functionality:
- Send generated PDF/book layout to print provider
- Handle order tracking (basic)

---

### 5.9 Share Page (Viral Loop)
After purchase:
- Display book preview
- Allow sharing link
- “Create yours” CTA for referrals

---

# 6. User Flow

1. Landing page
2. Click “Create Book”
3. Upload photos
4. Select style
5. AI generates coloring pages
6. Preview results
7. Customize book (title, cover)
8. Checkout
9. Order fulfillment

# 11. Risks

## 1. Low perceived value
Users may see this as “cute but not worth buying”

Mitigation:
- Strong emotional positioning
- Gift framing

---

## 2. Poor AI output quality
Bad line art reduces trust and conversions

Mitigation:
- Curated style presets
- Manual QA in MVP stage

---

## 3. Weak differentiation
Many similar tools exist

Mitigation:
- Focus on “gift experience” not “AI tool”
- Strong storytelling layer

---

# 12. Differentiation Strategy

This product MUST NOT be positioned as:

- ❌ AI coloring generator
- ❌ Photo-to-line-art tool

It MUST be positioned as:

- ✅ Personalized memory gift experience
- ✅ Custom story-based coloring books
- ✅ Emotional keepsake product

Optional advanced differentiation:
- Story Mode (AI generates captions per page)
- Character Mode (child/pet as recurring character)
- Occasion Mode (birthday, holiday, memorial books)
